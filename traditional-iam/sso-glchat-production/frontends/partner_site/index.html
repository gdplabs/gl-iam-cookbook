Welcome to the partner portal

This panel represents the partner's own application. A user logs in once with their partner credentials. On successful login, the embedded GLChat widget on the right automatically signs them into GLChat via IdP-initiated SSO — no second login, no redirect, no pop-up.

Topology simulated by this cookbook: partner site (localhost:3001) → partner backend (:8001) → GLChat backend (:8000) → GLChat widget iframe (:3003). The one-time SSO token travels server-to-server and is delivered to the widget via postMessage — never in the URL query string.
